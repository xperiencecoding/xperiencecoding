    Empowering Young Minds Through Code

    A non-profit based in the Lake County Area dedicated to to providing 2nd-8th graders with the foundational skills of coding.
    Our mission is to empower the youth of the greater Lake County Area through the world of coding.

    Some of the work that we have done...

    Our most recent camp was the first week of January 2024, where we fortunate to have ~40 kids, ages 8-14, sign up
    Our camp stretched over 4 days, with kids learning foundational skills for the first couple days, and then creating thier own game the last two days.
    We invited parents to come in on the last day and see what their kid had made.
